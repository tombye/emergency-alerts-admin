Send messages in bulk

You can send a batch of emails, text messages and letters.

Send up to 100,000 emails or text messages in one go. The daily limit for sending emails or text messages is 250,000.

Send up to 25,000 letters a day in one go. Contact us if you need to send more.

To send a batch of messages at once:

  1. Go to the {{ service_link(current_service, 'main.choose_template', 'templates') }} page.
  2. Add a new template or choose an existing template and selectGet ready to send.
  3. Select Upload a list of email addresses, Upload a list of phone numbers or Upload a list of addresses.

You can also choose when to send your messages. Find out [more about scheduling messages].
